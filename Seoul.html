Seoul is a bustling city with a fascinating natural landscape and a long history. It is the capital of South Korea. In my mind, Seoul is a 24 hours city that, whether you want anything, day or night, you can always find what you want in the city. I love watching Korean TV episodes so much. The food and views attract me. Those years, Korean POP stars play vital roles in the entertaining area. Some companies' buildings also become landmarks for the soul. It is a paradise for shopping. Besides, it is my first time traveling with my two sisters. The journey was so fascinating which left me an unforgettable impression.

Reasons I LOVE

  • Shopping Malls
  • Night Market
  • Bigbang

Shopping Mall

Seoul has numerous shopping malls. Some of them will open till midnight. I can shop from day to night. The clothes in Seoul are fashion but not expensive. Korean fashion style leads the world's fashion trend in nowadays society. Some Korean stars will also open their personal brand in Seoul. I have an amazing shopping experience there.

Night Market

Night Markets are one of the features of Seoul. Every night, numerous stands will appear along the streets. The food smells so good, attracting me to buy them. I can pick various food according to my preferences. All the local food is offered with small plates. Therefore, I no longer need to worry if I may miss some of the delicious plates due to the fullness of my stomach.

Bigbang

Bigbang is a South Korean boy band, formed by YG entertainment. The company is established in Seoul. Every year, Bigbang will hold many fan meetings in Seoul. Those are precious chances for fans to meet the idols. I have been a fan of this band for five years. This band attracts me to Seoul at first.
