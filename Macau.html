Macau

"One Of The Most Tempting Cities"

  • Macau Tower

When mentioning Macau, the most famous feature must be gambling, a city with countless casinos. However, my memory of it this city has no relation to it. Because of personal reason, my parents would take me to Macau twice a year. I witnessed Macau changed from a battered city to a bustling one. Macau has many attractions besides Casino, and there are delicious egg tarts, the famous Ruins of St. Paul, and the romantic Rose Hall. Streetside snack tea restaurants, historical museums with different themes, various cultural sites and European churches also attracts many tourists to come. Also, Macao is a dynamic city with many activities such as an annual International Food Festival, International Fireworks Festival, Grand Prix, Film Festival and colorful nightlife. No matter what kind of mentality I visited it, Macau will always give me the surprise.
