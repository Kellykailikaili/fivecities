Shanghai, an open city on the coast, is a gate to the Yangtze River delta. It is the most significant economic and trade center in China. It gathers various people consisting of a dynamic city. It is also the city that I live and grow. I spent most of the time living in this city. This city carries all my memory. It is a dynamic city for me that combines various culture and architecture.

Reasons I LOVE

  • Food

Food

Shanghai has countless restaurants. Everyone can choose the restaurants they like according to their taste. For me, I love Shanghai cuisine so much. The feature of Shanghai Cuisine is sweet. The Dim Sum is also the feature in Shanghai. Shanghai has the world famous Xiao Long Bao. I miss my home so much.

Night View

Shanghai has abundant architectures. Those mostly gather in Lujiazui area. Every night, various lights will be opened. I love chatting with my friends on the rooftop with my friends, viewing the night view. It is my happiest moment. Besides, when I feel upset, the Bund is the best place for me to calm down. The view and lights will let me feel relax and happy.

My School

It is my primary, middle and high school, Shanghai Pinghe Bilingual School. It was a place that I used to hate, but I missed there when I graduated. It is a place full of my childhood, witness my growth. I spent twelve years in there, meeting friends and learning knowledge. It is a boarding school that I need to sleep in school for five days a week. Therefore, I spent most of the days with my classmates rather than with my parents.
