Osaka is in the west side of Japan, a famous traveling city. Osaka is the second largest city in Japan, with long history. It is the center of a national economic, political and cultural. The commerce is prosperous and transportation is convenient. It is a popular city popular among domestic and foreign travelers. As a comprehensive international metropolis, Osaka is as same as Tokyo in terms of prosperity and fashion. It has high-rise buildings, developed transportation, and particularly prosperous business development. It is the most famous "food heaven" in Japan, which is the most attractive point for me.

Universal Studio

I am a crazy fan of the Minion. Osaka’s universal studio has world first minion theme park in it. I can see the well-designed of the area. There are minion theme rollercoaster and the 3D animation minion dancing in front of you. Also, there are so many minion products such as Lego and cups. I spent most of the day in that theme park. Besides, the food is also cute that all of them are related to the Minion. If you buy a churro in that theme park, the flavor should be banana and with a minion on the top. Besides that, there are other themes such as Harry Potter and Water Park. It is so worth to spend a whole day in there. I left the best memory there.

Food

The food is the most attractive reason for me to go to this place every year. There is various food that offers me to pick every day. It is my favorite portion called Shabu-shabu. It is a Japanese nabemono hotpot dish that consisted of sliced meat and vegetable. There are more than ten types of food in one hot pot. It is a little bit sweet but very delicious.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle, which took three years to build, was completed in 1593. Unfortunately, it was destroyed by Tokugawa’s army in 1615. The Osaka Castle now is a concrete building that was copied in 1931 and was rebuilt in 1997. There are a wealth of exhibits about Toyotomi Hideyoshi and Osaka. The best view is the time that cherries are blooming which is in early April. It is a place entirely different from the center of Osaka. The place is quiet and beautiful. I keep calm down to enjoy the exhibits and learn the history.
