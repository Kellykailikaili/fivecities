Quebec City is located in Quebec, Canada. It was the capital of New France in 1608. Unlike other North American cities, Quebec City is very small but exquisite. As the only English-French bilingual metropolis in North America, it is the second largest French city in the world. You can always see many featured European architectures. Waling in the street, you can imagine yourself being immersed in romantic French culture.

Views

I first knew this city is because of a Korean TV episode called Guardian: The Lonely and Great God. It is one of the most popular episodes in Asian in 2016. The scenes in the episodes attracted me.The view in Quebec City is so romantic. There are many castles and churches in Quebec City. Some of them are gothic style and some of the Catholic style. When I visited there, I just finished my theology class. It is so amazing to see those architectures with my eyes.

The Restaurants

Quebec City is a Mecca for gourmets. There are many French cuisines in Quebec City. All the dishes are delicious and delicate. The coffee shops are along the rive which can offer the customers enjoy not only the food but also the view. I no longer need to take a long-time plane to France but still can try proper French cuisine.

Simpleness

Different from the rest four cities I pick in this assignment. Quebec City is not as bustling as others, but it is the most comfortable city for me. Life in Quebec City is slow. It is a new feeling for me since I mostly live in Shanghai and New York, both of them are cosmopolitan cities. The city is small. I can drive half an hour to wine chateau. Listening to the local people telling their stories about how to make the wines. The city is small, but people are all with passion and welcome.
